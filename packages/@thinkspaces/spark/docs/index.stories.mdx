<h1>Welcome to Spark,<br />A simple design system</h1>

Spark provides components, design tokens, utilities and tools to help boost teams collaborations around design, engineering, and product. Usage of these resources will aim to provide simplicity in the workspace along with a basis for forming productive conversations across all sorts of experiences.
